Marlene Dumas was the source of inspiration for Workshop 01. I selected one of her ink illustrations as a beginning form, and from there let myself explore quite freely. Wet into wet. Energy over likeness. I repeated this process 3 times illustrating what you can see here.

Workshop 02 was looking at the principles of how one uses colour. We looked at Maira Kalman's work during class, but I didn't quite love what I was creating. Colour is used very liberally in Kalman's illustrations, and I wouldn't say I'm much of a 'colourist' myself. I'm particularly fond of using dark colours, minimalist colour schemes and the use of black in particular, so I decided to try again at home and using one of Eirian Chapman's fashion illustraions landed on this lady here.

Reference: Eirian Chapman
